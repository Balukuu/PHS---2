Who We Are

Uganda's Leading Pest Management Company

Public Health Solutions (U) Ltd. is a Uganda-registered subsidiary of Pangolin International, operating since 2010. Based at Plot 43, Bukoto Street, Kamwokya, Kampala, we are led by an international team skilled in public health and pest management.

Our company manages 17 professionals including spray representatives, spray supervisors, co-coordinators, technical managers, and office administrators — each fully trained in safe pest control practices and international chemical standards.

In 15 years of operation, we have grown to become the leading pest management provider across Uganda's hospitality, diplomatic, NGO, educational, and warehousing sectors — committed to raising international standards in an industry where quality is too often compromised.

We are proud to serve Uganda's most prestigious properties and organizations, providing discreet, professional service backed by WHO, EU, and US EPA certifications.
