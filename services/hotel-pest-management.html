Why Uganda's Best Hotels Choose PHS

Five-star hospitality demands pest control that is completely invisible to guests. No chemical smells during breakfast service. No stains on freshly painted walls. No disruption during check-in. PHS delivers precisely that — using ICON CS, the only microencapsulated pesticide in Uganda that leaves no odor, no staining, and near-zero toxicity footprint.

  • Zero odor — guests never detect service has occurred
  • Zero wall staining — no damage to decor or surfaces
  • Scheduled visits planned around hotel operations

Program Structure

The PHS Hotel Pest Management Program

Guest Rooms & Corridors

ICON CS Indoor Residual Spraying in all guest rooms, corridors, and common areas. Scheduled during room turnover — no inconvenience to guests.

Kitchen & Food Areas

HACCP-compatible gel bait systems for cockroach control — no chemical sprays near food preparation surfaces. Monthly bait replenishment and monitoring.

Bed Bug Prevention

Quarterly bed bug inspection and preventive treatment program. Discreet treatment methodology — laundry, mattress, and furniture treatment without room closure.

Rodent Control

Tamper-resistant rodent bait stations in service areas, back-of-house, and perimeter. Monthly inspection and restocking with full activity reporting.

Mosquito Management

Monthly ICON CS MMS program for guest rooms, outdoor dining areas, and hotel gardens. Critical for 5-star guest experience and health compliance.

Audit Documentation

Monthly service reports, chemical safety data sheets, and pest activity records — all formatted for international hotel audit requirements and food safety inspections.
