Warning Signs

Signs of Termite Infestation

Early detection is critical. Termites cause structural damage silently — often for months before visible signs appear.

Mud Tubes

Pencil-width tubes on walls, foundations, or wooden surfaces — termites' protective highways

Hollow Timber

Wood that sounds hollow when tapped — termites eat from the inside out, leaving a thin shell

Frass Deposits

Small piles of sawdust-like termite droppings near wood structures or window sills

Discarded Wings

Small wings near doors, windows, or light sources — signs of a recent termite swarm

Sagging Floors/Ceilings

Structural weakening from internal timber damage — advanced infestation indicator

Clicking Sounds

Quiet clicking sounds from walls — soldier termites banging heads to signal danger
