Our Fumigation Process

Site Assessment & Pest Survey

Our technical team conducts a comprehensive site inspection to assess infestation levels, commodity quantities, and structural integrity for fumigation.

Sealing & Preparation

The facility is sealed using appropriate sheets, tarpaulins, or containment systems to ensure fumigant retention at effective concentrations.

Fumigant Application

WHO-approved fumigants are applied at scientifically calculated dosage rates by our certified fumigation team in full PPE.

Exposure Period & Monitoring

Gas concentrations are monitored throughout the exposure period to ensure lethal doses are maintained for complete pest elimination.

Aeration & Clearance

Controlled aeration until gas concentrations drop to safe levels. Site is officially cleared before re-entry authorization is given.

Post-Fumigation Report & Certification

Full fumigation certificate and treatment report issued for regulatory compliance, export documentation, and client records.
