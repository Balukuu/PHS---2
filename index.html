Why PHS

Focused on safety, trusted by Uganda's most demanding institutions.

Since 2010 we have set the standard for certified pest management in East Africa. We use only WHO, EU, and US EPA approved chemicals, applied by trained and accountable teams. Every treatment is documented, discreet, and built around the safety of the people in the space.
